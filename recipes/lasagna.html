  • 1 oound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can of crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans of canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounc) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1.5 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 dasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1 egg
  1. Gather all you ingredients
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a dutch oven over medium heat until well browned
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomatoo paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teasppoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil (salt once water is boiling). Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 mminutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  7. To assemble, spread 1.5 cups of meat sauce in the bbottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1.5 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella dn Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil wiht cooking spray or make sure the foil doe snot touch the cheese
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foild and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  10. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving
